Summary

Dary Cain was born in Fresno, CA. After graduating from Bullard High School, Darcy attended the University of California, Santa Barbara where she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and Psychology. She began and accelerated her career at Varitalk , a small digital start up that specialized in viral digital marketing campaigns. Following Varitalk, she excelled as an Account Manager for a Creative Design Agency called Crew Creative Advertising . This job catapulted her to her most recent position as a Director in International Marketing and Publicity at Lionsgate Entertainment (fka Summit Entertainment) . At Summit and then Lionsgate, Darcy helped oversee the International releases of films including The Twilight Saga, The Hunger Games, 12 Years a Slave, and The Divergent Series. Darcy is now pursuing her MBA at the Kellogg School of Management.
