Summary

Jack Bauer is a CIA operative was born in Santa Monica, California on February 18, 1966. Jack has a Bachelor of Arts degree in English Literature from the University of California, Los Angeles and a Master of Science degree in Criminology and Law from the University of California, Berkeley. He then enlisted in the army. It was here that he demonstrated a strong proficiency with fire arms and flying airplanes. Jack's wife, Teri, was murdered by a traitor inside the CTU. This has led to a tumultuous relationship with his daughter, Kim.
